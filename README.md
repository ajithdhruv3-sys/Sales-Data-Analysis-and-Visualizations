# Sales-Data-Analysis-and-Visualizations

**Project Overview **

This project focuses on cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing a global sales dataset to uncover meaningful business insights. The dataset includes transactions across multiple countries, product categories, and brands, with fields such as purchase dates, prices, quantities, and total revenue.

📈** Key Insights**
1. Electronics dominate revenue
Electronics contribute $234,200, over 80% of total sales.

2. Canada & US are top markets
🇨🇦 Canada: $67,950

🇺🇸 USA: $62,300

3. Peak sales months
September: $61,000

June: $59,750

4. Top brands
HP – $47,500

Google – $36,000

Nikon – $35,000

5. Duplicate entries found
Bose Headphones (ES, 4/16/2025)

HP Laptop (IN, 6/17/2025)
